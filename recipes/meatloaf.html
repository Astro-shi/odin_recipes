Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian al forno (baked) dish
composed of alternating layers of wide, flat pasta sheets,
savory ragù (meat sauce), creamy white béchamel or ricotta cheese,
and melted mozzarella or Parmesan. It is a hearty, layered casserole
typically baked until bubbly, golden, and set.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • ⅔ cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 slices bread, crumbled
  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup shredded carrot
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  3. Repeat Layers: Repeat the sequence (noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, sauce) until the dish is filled, ending with a final layer of meat sauce and a generous amount of mozzarella on top.
  4. Bake: Cover with foil (spray it first to prevent sticking) and bake for 30 minutes; remove foil and bake another 15–20 minutes until bubbling and cheese is golden.
  5. Rest: Let the lasagna rest for 15–20 minutes before serving to ensure clean slices
